Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Foolder (“we”, “us”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Foolder app and related services.

Information we collect

  • Account information: email address and encrypted authentication credentials used to create and access your account.
  • Usage data: content library, watch progress, and app settings that you save in your account.
  • Technical data: basic logs necessary to operate and secure the service (for example IP address and request metadata).

How we use information

  • Provide account sign-in and synchronize your library and progress.
  • Operate, maintain, and secure the service.
  • Respond to support requests.

Sharing and third parties

We use third‑party services to provide the app experience, such as authentication and database hosting (Supabase) and metadata providers (TMDB/AniList). The app may also connect to external content/addon providers you configure.

Data retention

We keep your account data while your account is active. You can request deletion at any time.

Account deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated data here: Request account deletion.

Security

We use industry‑standard measures to protect your information and encrypt data in transit where supported by the service infrastructure.

Children’s privacy

Foolder is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Contact

If you have questions, contact us at support@foolder.tv.
